pinned baselines · honest signal

Two corpora, three numbers.

The headline 100% is the heuristic against questions it was tuned on — useful for catching regressions, not for proving generalization. The held-out split paraphrases each question with vocabulary the heuristic has never seen, so we can see how each backend behaves on novel input. Both numbers are real and pinned in tests/fixtures/.
